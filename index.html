Marcus Peabody

is a product and editorial designer. He was most recently the Creative Director & head of visuals at the Guardian US, with previous stints at the Wall Street Journal, New York Magazine, and the New York Times. He works on editorial design, editorial product and branding projects, art direction, photography, and news graphics. He studied at the Glasgow School of Art and the Rhode Island School of Design, and has taught at the Parsons School of Design and RISD. Get in touch at marcus.peabody@me.com
