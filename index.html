Marcus Peabody                 
Marcus Peabody is an editorial product designer at New York Magazine, and a teacher at the Parsons School of Design.

He works on digital editorial design in all its forms: elaborate interactive visual story formats, hard working typographic components, and editorial visuals and graphics.

He studied at the Rhode Island School of Design, and the Glasgow School of Art.
