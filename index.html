Marcus Peabody                 
Marcus Peabody is an editorial product designer at New York Magazine.

He works on digital editorial design in all its forms: elaborate interactive visual story formats, typographic components, homescreens, and editorial visuals, maps and graphics.

He studied at the Glasgow School of Art, and teaches part time at the Parsons School of Design.
